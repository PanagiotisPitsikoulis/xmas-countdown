Instagram account, they are
supporting the company by staying
up to date with their latest
products, services, and events.
This can help to increase
engagement and interest in the
startup's offerings, which can
ultimately lead to increased
support to the services provided.

GitHub interactions can also
lead to improved code quality,
by allowing developers to
review and contribute to the
codebase, so a startup team

reliability of their product.

arise and increase customer
satisfaction.
